Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-17

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the PauliusCVApp handles information.
The App is published by Paulius Urbaitis.

Data We Collect

  • Profile photo (optional): If you take a photo, it is stored only on your device.
  • CV and portfolio entries (optional): Text and image URLs you add are stored only on your device.
  • Contact form submission (optional): Your name (if provided), contact details, and message are placed into an email draft addressed to pauliusurbaitis@gmail.com. The App does not send the email automatically.

How We Use Data

  • Profile photo: Displayed inside the App.
  • CV and portfolio entries: Displayed to personalize your card.
  • Contact form: Used to create an email draft that you may choose to send.

Data Sharing

  • Email: If you choose to send the draft, its contents are transmitted by your email app to pauliusurbaitis@gmail.com.
  • Remote images: Portfolio images are loaded from the URLs you provide. Requests are sent directly to the hosting services (for example, Unsplash).
  • We do not share data with any other third parties.

Storage & Retention

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Uninstalling the App or using in-app delete/reset options removes locally stored data.
  • Emails you send are stored by your email provider under their policies.
  • We do not store data on external servers.

Permissions

  • Camera (optional): Used only to capture a profile photo.
  • Internet: Used only to load portfolio images from the internet.
  • No other permissions are required.

Your Choices & Controls

  • You may delete CV items, portfolio entries, and your profile photo at any time.
  • You may uninstall the App to remove all local data.
  • You may decline to send the contact form.

Security

All App data remains on your device. Email transmission is handled by your chosen email app.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates to this policy will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions, contact: pauliusurbaitis@gmail.com
